About This Archive

These tools take you through the end of the Golden Age - the final years of Pan Am, Eastern and TWA - through the beginning of post-9/11 consolidation, the birth of superhubs and the regional jet takeover. See the coverage table below for more details on what's included.

This database was compiled by digitizing original airline timetables - physical booklets that listed every scheduled flight in the system - along with data from the Official Airline Guide (OAG) originally digitized on DepartedFlights.com.

Visit DepartedFlights.com to view supplemental materials such as route maps, additional timetables, aircraft photos, and original data.
